Posadas leads the way in sustainable waste management

With 11 fixed ecopuntos, 9 Puntos Limpios, and a growing circular economy, Posadas and Misiones province recycled nearly 24,000 tonnes of urban waste in 2024 — generating an estimated $8.5 billion pesos in economic activity. Here's why your contribution matters.

♻️
Circular economy
Recycled materials re-enter production chains — paper becomes egg cartons in Candelaria, plastics are reprocessed in Alem.
🌫️
Lower emissions
Diverting waste from landfill cuts greenhouse gas emissions and reduces the environmental footprint of Posadas.
💼
Green jobs
Urban waste collectors (recuperadores urbanos) earn income by classifying and selling recyclables at official collection centres.
🏫
Education
Schools across Misiones became active ecopuntos, teaching students to separate waste at home and earn resources for their classrooms.
🌲
Natural resources
Recycling reduces the extraction of raw materials, protecting Misiones' unique forests and rich biodiversity.
🏙️
Cleaner city
Mobile ecopuntos tour city neighbourhoods, keeping streets clean and giving every resident easy access to recycling services.
